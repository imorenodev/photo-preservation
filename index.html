Frequently Asked Questions

We bring professional scanning equipment directly to your home. All work is done safely in your living space, and your photos never leave your house. We handle thousands of photos efficiently while you relax at home.

We can scan all types of photos including vintage prints, Polaroids, framed photos, albums, and even damaged or faded photos. Our professional equipment handles various sizes from wallet photos to large family portraits.

For our in-home service, we typically scan 500-1000 photos per hour depending on size and condition. Most jobs are completed in one visit. For pickup and delivery service, turnaround is usually 1-2 weeks.

We scan all photos at high resolution (typically 600-1200 DPI) to ensure maximum quality and detail preservation. This allows for enlargements and ensures your digital copies look crisp on any device.

Yes! We offer professional photo enhancement services including color restoration for faded photos, colorization of black and white images, and repair of torn or damaged photographs. We can also convert static photos into engaging video presentations.

We serve Denville, NJ and surrounding areas within a 25-mile radius. This includes Morris County and nearby communities. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Photos are organized by date, event, or your preferred system. We provide your digitized photos on USB drives, through a private family website, and can set up digital frames. You also get secure long-term cloud storage access.

No minimum required! We offer a $50 starter package for 50 scans to try our service. Our packages accommodate anywhere from 50 photos to over 10,000 photos depending on your needs.
